What is QuaRunTime 2.0?

QuaRunTime was the first event organized by the Computer Society Chapter of IEEE AJCE SB which was formed last year. The 6-day event had an active participation from over 250+ participants. This year, we are back with the second version of our flagship event, QuaRunTime 2.0 with 6 different eventd,which will run from 7th June to 12th June, 2021.
