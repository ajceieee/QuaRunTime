What is QuaRunTime 2.0?

QuaRunTime was the first event organized by the Computer Society Chapter of IEEE AJCE SB which was formed last year. The 6-day event had an active participation from over 250+ participants. This year, we are back with the second version of our flagship event, QuaRunTime 2.0 with 6 different events,which will run from 7th June to 12th June, 2021.

Events

All the events for QuaRunTime 2.0 are listed below.

  • Quiz - 7th June

  • Word Search - 8th June

  • Cryptography - 9th June

  • Crossword - 10th June

  • Aptitude - 11th June

  • Treasure Hunt - 12th June
